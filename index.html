MLS Quick Reference

Turnaround

Same-day goal  ·  48-hour maximum from completed questionnaire. Significant delays may require a new listing agreement.

VPN

Always on with Miami exit node before accessing any MLS. If you cannot connect, stop and message Yaelyn before proceeding.

Queue Statuses

StatusMeaning
NewSeller completed questionnaire — unassigned
For ReviewBlocked — photos, floor plan, ownership issue, MLS issue
In ProgressRep self-assigned, actively working — do not touch
IncompleteSeller must log in and fix or upload something
Automation ErrorAutomation stuck/failed — report to #Tech on Cliq
Active / ListedSuccessfully published — no action needed

First Photo Rule

Must be front exterior, rendering, aerial, or water view only. A swimming pool does NOT qualify as a water view.

Photo Minimums

Most MLS residential: 3  ·  HAR (Houston): 6  ·  Land (most MLS): 1  ·  NTREIS: 1

Absolutely Forbidden Types

Auction  ·  Tax Lien Sale  ·  Foreclosure  ·  Remainderman's right — no exceptions, no packages.

Wholesale Forbidden States

IL, SC, DC, MA, MD, NJ, NY, OK, PA, RI, UT, WI (by state law). Ohio: allowed only if agreement signed by all parties.

Ownership Signatures

ScenarioSign as
Owner is a Trust"Seller for Trust name"
Owner is LLC / Corp"Seller, Member/Manager" or "Seller for Company name"
Two ownersBoth must sign
Owner deceased (Estate)"Estate of Owner, by Seller, Personal Representative"

NAR Compensation Rule

No agent compensation or commission in ANY MLS field — ever. No description, remarks, or any other field.
